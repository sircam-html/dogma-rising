Scheduled Maintenance Announcement

There will be a maintenance in 9 hours with an expected downtime of up to 15 minutes.

I apologize for the unscheduled maintenance!
I had previously promised scheduled restarts only, however due to limited availability next week I need to push this patch to this Sunday to ensure the best experience for everyone while I am away.

Maintenance Patch Notes:

Party fixes!

• Fixed an issue where the inviter could be kicked from their own party when an invite timed out or was cancelled, requiring both players to relog

• Fixed an issue where re-inviting a player after they disconnected would fail with an error stating a pending invite already existed

• Fixed an issue where a party invite slot would remain permanently stuck if the inviter disconnected before the invite expired

• Fixed an issue where cancelling an invite that had already expired would cause a server error

• Fixed an issue where two players inviting each other simultaneously could result in both ending up in a broken party state.

Note: When both players invite each other at the same time, neither will receive an invite popup to accept. You can cancel the pending invitation via the party management menu and invite again normally

• Fixed an issue where joining a party while an invite was pending would show the invited player as a ghost member in your party UI

• Fixed an issue where a pending invitee who declined or timed out remained visible as a ghost party member to existing members

• Fixed an issue where a player's pawns remained in the party UI of other members after the player left or disconnected

Known Issues

Thank you for your understanding and have a nice Sunday!
