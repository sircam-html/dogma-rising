March 29, 2026 Jared 📸 Contest

Dogma Rising Screenshot Contest!

Hello everyone! Ahead of the upcoming maintenance, we have an exciting community event to announce!

We are launching the Dogma Rising Screenshot Contest with two separate categories: Modded and Unmodded. Each category has its own dedicated submission channel in our Discord server.

IMPORTANT: Please only post your entries in the designated Submissions channels.

Rules and Information:

• One entry per person. You may replace your entry at any time by simply deleting your previous submission.

• The contest runs until the end of April 2026. After submissions close, we will organize the entries and open a community poll that will last two weeks.

• Unmodded Category: No ReShade, Photoshop, HD Mod, or any visual-altering modifications allowed. Only the EXE for chat limit / frame rate is permitted.

• Modded Category: ReShade, HD mods, and any in-game visual enhancements are allowed. External editing (Photoshop, etc.) is strictly prohibited.

• You may submit any screenshot you've taken in Dogma Rising — landscapes, epic battles, character creations, or unique moments are all welcome!

Rewards:

6 winners total (3 modded + 3 unmodded). Winners will receive a special Discord role "Pawntographer" (1st, 2nd, or 3rd place) and exclusive in-game items: Victor's Crowns (Item IDs 13831, 13832, 13833).
