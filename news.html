Dogma Rising Screenshot Contest!

Hello everyone! Ahead of maintenance, I have an announcement!

We have 2 categories, modded and unmodded, with their respective channels.

PLEASE ONLY POST IMAGES IN THE SUBMISSIONS CHANNEL.



Rules and Info:



• One entry per person. If you choose to change your entry, you are more than welcome to, simply delete your previous message. At the end of the contest time, I will go through every submission personally to make sure we do not have duplicates.

• The contest will run until the end of April. Submissions will be organized thereafter, then a poll will be made. The poll will last for two weeks so that everyone has a chance to vote, then the winners will be announced!

• When the contest closes, I will react to every image with a number for its contest entry. This will coincide with a poll (probably a strawpoll due to expected entry numbers) that will be listed with your discord name next to it in the selection

• You may enter into both the modded and unmodded categories, but you are only able to win one. If you place Top 3 in both, the lower spot of the 2 will be skipped. If you somehow place the exact same in both, the skipped category for you will be chosen at random.

• For the UNMODDED category, this means no ReShade, Photoshop, HD Mod, or any other mod that changes the fidelity of the game. The ONLY allowed modification will be the EXE for chat limit/frame rate.

• Yes, we understand that the base game's graphics aren’t the best, but not everyone is able to get the graphical modifications running on their system and it needs to be fair.

• For the MODDED category, you may use ReShade, the HD mod, or any other mod that changes the visual fidelity INSIDE THE GAME. No photoshop or other outside modifications

• If we suspect any vote rigging or other manipulation outside the rules, we reserve the right to take appropriate action.

• You may post any screenshot you’ve taken! You don’t need to take a new one for the competition!

Now for those juicy rewards:



To keep it simple, we’re going to have 6 winners total. 3 from the modded category and 3 from unmodded will be crowned winners! Literally!

A special discord role will be given plus an item in game.
The role will be 1st, 2nd, or 3rd place Pawntographer.
The items in the game will be the Victor’s Crowns. Item ID 13831, 13832, and 13833. (Pics coming soon ™)

We’ve been seeing some great screenshots from everyone and look forward to what you come up with.

You can find a copy paste of this info in discord #contest-info

March 28, 2026 | LostMyLeg

Scheduled Maintenance Announcement

There will be maintenance on Sunday, March 29, at 7:00 AM, with expected downtime of up to 15 minutes.

I apologize for the unscheduled maintenance!
I had previously promised scheduled restarts only, however due to limited availability next week I need to push this patch to this Sunday to ensure the best experience for everyone while I am away.

Maintenance Patch Notes:

Party fixes!

• Fixed an issue where the inviter could be kicked from their own party when an invite timed out or was cancelled, requiring both players to relog

• Fixed an issue where re-inviting a player after they disconnected would fail with an error stating a pending invite already existed

• Fixed an issue where a party invite slot would remain permanently stuck if the inviter disconnected before the invite expired

• Fixed an issue where cancelling an invite that had already expired would cause a server error

• Fixed an issue where two players inviting each other simultaneously could result in both ending up in a broken party state.

Note: When both players invite each other at the same time, neither will receive an invite popup to accept.

• Fixed an issue where joining a party while an invite was pending would show the invited player as a ghost member

• Fixed an issue where a pending invitee remained visible as a ghost party member

• Fixed an issue where a player's pawns remained in the party UI after leaving

Known Issues

Thank you for your understanding and have a nice Sunday!
