About Us

Discover Dogma Rising

Dogma Rising is a community-run private server for Dragon's Dogma Online (DDON), a Capcom MMORPG that officially shut down in 2019. As of March 2026, it is actively maintained and remains one of the most popular ways to experience the game with modern English translations and gameplay improvements.
